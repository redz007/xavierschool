Morals and Right Conduct

At Xavier's PreSchool we prioritize and value teaching kids about good morals and right conduct. We believe that these two basic traits can help them be a good person of society and be model adults as they grow up.

Our Vision
We envision developing a future generation of leaders who value global well-being and approach their life challenges with integrity, resiliency and compassion.

Our Mission
The Xaiver Preschool is focused on providing a fun, nurturing and curiosity-driven environment where children develop a love for learning and a grateful perspective on their lives.

Our Goal
Our goal is to nurture curiosity, strengthen resiliency, build social bonds and cultivate appreciation for the world around us so that young minds can thrive.
